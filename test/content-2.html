The Skinny Shot at Mia Aesthetics

At Mia Aesthetics, we believe in transparency when it comes to your treatments. Our Skinny Shots contain a powerful medication paired with pyridoxine—commonly known as vitamin B6. B-vitamins act as lipotropes, helping your liver reduce fat production and storage. Additionally, vitamin B can provide an energy boost. Keep in mind that while the Skinny Shot is a helpful tool for weight loss, it works best when combined with a balanced diet and regular exercise routine.

Common Skinny Shot Components

Different doctors refer to different injections as “skinny shots,” and there is no industry standard. A Skinny Shot could be anything from a vitamin injection to a prescription medication. Some doctors even claim to have their own special cocktail.

The Proprietary Mix

Weight loss is big business, which makes some practitioners reluctant to divulge their “secret” formulas. Avoid them. You deserve to know exactly what’s being injected into your body.

Vitamin B12

Vitamin B12 injections help treat deficiency and reduce fatigue—but only if you’re actually deficient. They are not stand-alone weight-loss solutions.

Lipotropic Injections (Lipo-C)

Lipotropes encourage the liver to metabolize fat. Research is still inconclusive on safety and efficacy, and ingredient mixes vary by clinic.

Know What You’re Getting

Always demand full ingredient transparency before injection. Talk with your doctor to confirm each component is safe for you.

Know Who Is Giving It to You

Ensure your Skinny Shot is administered by a qualified medical professional—doctor, licensed dietician, or registered nurse—whose credentials you can verify.
